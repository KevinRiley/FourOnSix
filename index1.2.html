We provide Southwest Virginia with music for wedding ceremonies and receptions as well as corporate functions and other special events of various sizes. We service the Virginia counties of Montgomery and Giles as well as Pulaski, Dublin, Salem, Roanoke, and the Bluefield, WV area.

Four On Six Music offers a personalized music service catering to weddings, receptions, cocktail hours, and the like. The proprietors, Kevin and Verena Riley, are a husband and wife team who will work with you personally to ensure the music is just right for your wedding, dinner engagement or other special occasion. We have many years of experience performing at countless functions, concerts, and other events. We are not agents or brokers, but musicians who will personally attend to your requests.

Have a look around our website, and if you still have questions feel free to contact us.
